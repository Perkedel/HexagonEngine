Rekan2 silakan dipelajari materi ttg DFD, dan ERD. 
1. Pengertian DFD: a technique that diagrams the business processes and the data that pass among them 
2. Tahapan/Tingkatan DFD: Context Diagram, Level 0, Level 1, Level 2, dst 
3. Pengertian ERD: a picture which shows the information that is created, stored, and used by a business system 
4. Ada 3 komponen utama ERD: Entity, Atribut, Relationship 
5. Gunakan tool yang tersedia di internet untuk membuat DFD dan ERD. Tool paling umum adalah Microsoft Visio
